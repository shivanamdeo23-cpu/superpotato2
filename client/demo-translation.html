Important Health Notice

Asian women are at higher risk for osteoporosis due to genetic factors, body frame size, and dietary patterns. Early detection and prevention are crucial for maintaining bone health.

Clinical Research Evidence

Osteoporosis Risk: Asian women generally have lower bone mineral density (BMD) than other groups, placing them at higher risk for developing osteoporosis. This prevalence is higher, particularly among postmenopausal women.

Source: Lo JC, Pressman AR, et al. Osteoporosis and fracture among older US Asian adults. Curr Osteoporos Rep. 2023;21(4):374-383. PubMed PMID: 37542683
